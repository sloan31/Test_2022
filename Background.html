During this time I also found the sport of rock climbing. I joined the CSU climbing team and quickly found bouldering to be my favorite discpline.
Bouldering entails climbing rocks up to 20 feet with nothing but a pad below the boulder for protection.This allows the climber to move
free and perform arcobatic moves. Though I competed in college I've always found climbing outside to be my favorite form of climbing.
